# 🌌 State of Chaos: Technical White Paper (MVP)

**Version:** 1.8.0-mvp  
**Project:** State of Chaos  
**Token Ticker:** $SOC  
**Architecture:** Ethereum L2 (EVM)  
**Core Engine:** Generative AI Game Master (AGM)

---

## 1. Executive Summary
**State of Chaos** is a living grand strategy space simulation. The core engine is a **Generative AI Game Master (AGM)** that interprets **Player Context** to drive a reactive universe. In this ecosystem, **$SOC** is the energy of evolution, used to upgrade civilization pillars and execute strategic actions in an environment defined by extreme resource scarcity and AI-driven discovery.

---

## 2. The Gameplay Loop: "The Cycle of Chaos"

The game revolves around a tight, deflationary economic loop. **Special Actions** are the triggers that move the player through this cycle:

1.  **Evolve ($SOC):** Spend **$SOC** to upgrade the 5 Civilization Pillars.
2.  **Generate:** Pillars automatically produce Gold, Water, Oil, and Energy based on their levels.
3.  **Act (Special Actions):** Players execute **Attack**, **Spy On**, or **Missions** to interact with the galaxy.
4.  **AI Interpretation:** The AGM processes the **Context** of these actions to generate outcomes (Discovery, Sabotage, or War).
5.  **Trade & Rebuild:** Exchange resources for $SOC in the market to repair losses or fund the next cycle of evolution.

---

## 3. The 5 Pillars (The Context Framework)

Upgrading these pillars with **$SOC** increases the "Context Power" the AI uses to resolve player actions.

* **Infrastructure:** Base production of Gold, Oil, and Water.
* **Research (Internal):** Energy efficiency and finding resources *inside* your borders.
* **Intelligence (External):** The ability to "see" beyond your planet.
* **Defense:** Security of assets and counter-intelligence.
* **Fleet:** Power projection and cargo capacity.

---

## 4. Special Actions (User-Driven Context)

These are the specific commands players use to engage with the AGM and the Gameplay Loop.

### ⚔️ Attack
* **Context:** Fleet Pillar + Tactical Narrative.
* **Action:** Launch an offensive against a rival planet or NPC node.
* **Outcome:** Looting of resources and permanent $SOC burn for destroyed units.

### 🕵️ Spy On
* **Context:** Intelligence Pillar vs. Rival Defense Pillar.
* **Action:** Target a player or coordinates to gather data.
* **Outcome:** * **Find New Resources:** Locate rare deposits on other planets.
    * **Defense Audit:** Reveal a player’s exact fleet and shield status.

### 🛰️ Missions
* **Context:** Research + Fleet Pillars.
* **Action:** Send expeditions into deep space.
* **Outcome:** **Discovery.** The AI may spawn new resource nodes or unlock specialized tech (Infrastructure/Fleet blueprints) previously hidden.

---

## 5. Resource Scarcity & AI Discovery
Resources are **strictly limited** at the start. The map only expands when players use **Missions** or **Spy On** actions. 
* **Intelligence Discovery:** Finding a resource on another planet via spying may unlock the ability to build specific "Extractor" Infrastructure or "Heavy Raider" Fleets.
* **Research Discovery:** Finding a resource via internal missions unlocks "Refinery" tiers for your own planet.

---

## 6. Tokenomics: The $SOC Power
* **The Upgrade Sink:** Leveling up any Pillar burns $SOC.
* **The Action Fee:** Executing a Special Action (Spy/Mission) requires a small $SOC fee to process the AI context.
* **The Combat Burn:** 70% of $SOC invested in destroyed ships is burned; 30% becomes lootable "Scrap."

---

1.  **Phase 1:** $SOC Token & Planet NFT Launch.
2.  **Phase 2:** Upgrade System for the 5 Pillars (Infrastructure to Intelligence).
3.  **Phase 3:** Integration of **Special Actions** (Attack, Spy, Missions) within the Loop.
